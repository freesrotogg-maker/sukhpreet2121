Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 7, 2026. Learn how we prioritize GDPR-compliant data security.

1. Zero Database Collection Policy

We strongly believe in complete consumer anonymity. Our Site maintains absolutely NO server-side databases, login authentication sequences, or active user profiling trackers. Any items, retail stores, or attraction schedules that you bookmark inside our wayfinding planner are processed exclusively inside your browser’s local client-side memory (standard localStorage cache indices). We never transmit, share, or store this structural data outwards.

2. Support Mail Metrics

If you send support inquiries using our physical contact desk form, the name, email, and inquiry variables are compiled solely to address issues relative to database correction lists or business inclusion proposals. These messages are processed inside highly secure corporate email gateways and are deleted cleanly after 90 days of complete issue resolution. We never sell contact information under any circumstances.

3. Web Analytical Counters

To monitor high-level traffic scaling structures and optimize speed performances, the Site may utilize lightweight, anonymous click metrics (e.g., standard Google Analytics). These tools record simple geolocation indexes, timing intervals, and landing pathways without extracting any direct personally identifiable information (PII).

4. Rights of International Users (GDPR)

In full accordance with GDPR and CCPA compliance directives, visitors hold comprehensive rights over their data. Because we record zero remote metrics, you have total control over deleting your saved map coordinates at any time by simply clicking the "Trash" controls inside the Saved Directory Matrix, or clearing your cookies.
